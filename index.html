Our Heritage

Three Generations of Silk Excellence

Founded in 1985, Ananda Silks has been a trusted name in Karur for premium quality silk sarees. Our family has been in the silk business for over three generations, bringing you the finest handloom and powerloom silk sarees from across India.

We specialize in Kanchipuram silk, Banarasi silk, Mysore silk, and other traditional varieties, each carefully selected for its quality, craftsmanship, and authentic designs.

Our commitment to preserving traditional weaving techniques while embracing modern designs has made us a favorite among silk connoisseurs in Tamil Nadu and beyond.
